Game Rules & Terms

Team Play

Work together with your teammates to score goals and defend your net. Communication and coordination are key to success.

Physics-Based

Realistic ball physics and movement for authentic gameplay experience. Master the controls to become a skilled player.

Fair Play

Respect other players, no cheating, and maintain good sportsmanship. Toxic behavior will result in penalties.

Match Duration

Each match lasts 5 minutes with sudden death overtime if tied. Quick, action-packed gameplay.

Controls

Use WASD to move, Space to jump, and Mouse to aim. Practice makes perfect!

Team Formation

Teams are formed automatically. You can invite friends to join your team or play with random players.
